What is Inline Tag & Block Tag?
Inline tags means it takes the space according to their content.
Block tag means it will take a row space content doesn't matter.

What is Table tag?
Table tag means combination of rows & columns.

table tag
    tr tag (table row)
        td/th tag (td: table data & th: table head)
Table consits of two attributes
1. Cellspacing : space b/w outer cell & inner cell
2. Cellpadding : space b/w content & cell

RowMerge & ColumnMerge
1. rowspan: where you see any vertical merge.
2. Colspan : where you see any horizonta merge

Linking In HTML

target attribute helps us to open up the link in a new tab.
